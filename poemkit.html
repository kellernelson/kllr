Poetry Kit:

Meters(I-VIII)

  1. Monometer (one foot)
  2. Dimeter (two feet)
  3. Trimeter (three feet)
  4. Tetrameter (four feet)
  5. Pentameter (five feet)
  6. hexameter (six feet)
  7. heptameter (seven feet)
  8. octameter (eight feet)

Each poetic foot is composed of two syllables; any given syllable may be either stressed or unstressed.

Accentual-syllabic Meter:

  1. Iambic - (unstressed syllable)(stressed syllable)
  2. Trochaic - (stressed syllable)(unstressed syllable)
  3. Anapestic - (unstressed syllable)(unstressed syllable)(stressed syllable)
  4. Dactylic - (stressed syllable)(unstressed syllable)(unstressed syllable)
  5. Spondaic - two successive syllables similarly stressed.
  6. Pyrrhic - (unstressed syllable)(unstressed syllable)
