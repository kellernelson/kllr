~ Crash With Me ~

The wonder of the wave

is the wonder of a kiss--

the feeling of being loved

by someone that you miss.

/

But the beauty isn't the crash--

not the kiss at all--

but the vacuum that the wave creates

when it stands so tall;

/

That barrier of silence between you

and the rest of the water,

/

That serene stillness that

winds your hearts tauter.

/

And so the beauty isn't the kiss,

but those still moments of bliss.

/

The holding of breath,

the vulnerability, the creath;

/

That loving expanse

where hands and eyes dance--

/

Where wonders trance

and hearts lance...

/

The beauty before that

booming splash...

/

The beauty before that

roaring crash...

~ How can I tell you? ~

7 - I don't know how to tell you

7 - About how this all began.

7 - On that night when we both cried

7 - You reached out and grabbed my hand.

/

7 - I don't know how to tell you

7 - Of the things that went unsaid.

7 - In the end my brain is fried,

7 - My swallowed words sink like lead.

/

7 - I don't know how to tell you

7 - Of the things I hope we do.

7 - When my heart of hope subsides,

7 - I'll pray that thesee things come true.

/

7 - I don't know how to tell you

7 - Of the songs you make me sing.

7 - Come the day those old hymes die,

7 - Their words will forever ring.
